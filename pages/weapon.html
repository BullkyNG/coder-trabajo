Weapon List

  1. Greatswords

    Heavy damaging weapon, slow with great poise damage, not a great moveset

  2. Greataxes

    Heavy damaging weapon, slow with high poise damage and great recovery, good jumping attack

  3. Bows

    Long distance weapon, really efective against casting opponents comes in 3 different types (Long - Medium - Short)

  4. Crossbows

    Long distance weapon, faster than a bow but also less precise

  5. Spears

    Spears are really fast and do a lot of thrusting damage, they are better when power stanced

  6. Greatspears

    Big boy version of the spear, stronger poise damage, slower moveset

  7. Collosals

    Slowest weapon type in the game, comes with a great damage but a really hard to land moveset

  8. Thrusting Weapons

    Rapiers or thrusting weapons are really strong and fast, but the poise damage they do is mediocre

  9. Heavy Thrusting Weapons

    Big boy version of rapiers, really fast with an unparriable running r2, supers trong in pvp

  10. Straightswords

    Short swords, bad on their own, really good when power stancing the jumping L2 does great damage and is really fast
